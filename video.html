Sometimes even hundreds of still photos are not quite enough to catch the essence of a subject or place! To me, still photos and videos complement each other. While still photos provide the perfect framing and snapshot of a moment I notice, videos can provide a more comprehensive picture of the subject or place, both in the moment and in retrospect. Some of the memories that stand out to me are the ones I captured on video, such as being in my backyard with my tripod and telephoto lens, recording behaviors of the pretty red-headed woodpeckers I love. Thus, I am sharing a gallery of my favorite videos from home and from my travels.
